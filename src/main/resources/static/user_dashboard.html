Donor Health Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water before and after donation to help your body recover quickly.

Eat Iron-Rich Foods

Include foods like spinach, red meat, and beans in your diet to maintain healthy iron levels.

Rest Properly

Get a good night's sleep before donation and avoid strenuous activity for 24 hours after.
